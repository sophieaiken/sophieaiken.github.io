Curriculum Vitae

Hey! I'm Sophie Aiken, a 3rd year graduate student in mathematics at the University of California, Santa Cruz.

My research interests lie broadly in geometric analysis and mathematical physics. My advisor is Jie Qing. I am interested in the accessibility of gateway math courses and believe math prerequisites should not hold students back from excelling in their chosen fields.

Before coming to UC Santa Cruz I received my M.S. in Mathematics from the Higher School of Economics, where I wrote my master's thesis under Vadim Vologodsky; and my B.S. in Mathematics, with a specialization in applied algebra, from Shiv Nadar University.
