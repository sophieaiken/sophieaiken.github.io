Reading Group on A Course in Arithmetic by J.-P.Serre

Winter & Summer 2020

This is a reading group convened to read A Course in Arithmetic by J.-P.Serre. Our primary goal is to read and understand the first part of the book: Algebraic Methods. To that end we organize weekly seminars where the regular participants present sections from the book.

Regular Participants: Deewang Bhamidipati (Convener), Malachi Alexander, Sandra Nair
