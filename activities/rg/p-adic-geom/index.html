Reading Group on \(p\)-adic Geometry

Summer & Fall 2021

This is a reading group convened to learn about Rigid Analytic Spaces and Berkovich Spaces, the former and latter being individual focuses of the two participants. We are reading Rigid Analytic Geometry and Its Applications by Jean Fresnel, Marius van der Put and Berkovich Spaces and Applications; edited by Antoine Ducros, Charles Favre, Johannes Nicaise and holding weekly seminars.

Participants/Converners: Deewang Bhamidipati, Malachi Alexander
