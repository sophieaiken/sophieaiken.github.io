Reading Group on Étale Cohomology

Fall 2021

This is a reading group, organized as an Independent Study course with Prof. Junecue Suh. We will be reading the book Étale Cohomology by J.S. Milne. Previous iterations of this over the Summer has us reading through Etale Cohomology Theory by Lei Fu and Introduction to Étale Cohomology by Günter Tamme.
